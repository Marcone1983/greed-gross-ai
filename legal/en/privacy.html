1. Introduction

This Privacy Policy describes how GREED & GROSS ("we", "our" or "the App") collects, uses, stores and protects users' personal information. Our commitment is to ensure maximum transparency and protection of personal data in compliance with the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR), California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA), and other applicable laws.

3. Data We Collect

3.1 Data provided directly by the user:

  • Registration data: Username, email address, avatar (optional)
  • User preferences: Language, theme (light/dark), notification settings
  • Conversation data: Queries sent to the AI chatbot, feedback on responses
  • Subscription data: Premium status, transaction history (via RevenueCat)

3.2 Data collected automatically:

  • Usage data: Access frequency, features used, session duration
  • Technical data: Device type, operating system, app version
  • Analytics: In-app events, performance, crash reports (via Firebase Analytics)
  • Identifiers: Firebase Instance ID, anonymized User ID

4. Purpose of Processing

Personal data is processed for the following purposes:

  • Service provision: User account management, experience personalization, AI query processing
  • Service improvement: Usage analysis, AI algorithm optimization, new feature development
  • Communications: Push notifications about updates, new strains, offers (only with consent)
  • Support: Technical assistance, problem resolution, request management
  • Security: Fraud prevention, account protection, data backup
  • Legal compliance: Regulatory compliance, dispute management

5. Legal Basis

Data processing is based on:

  • Contract: Necessary to provide requested services
  • Consent: For marketing communications and certain cookies
  • Legitimate interest: To improve services and ensure security
  • Legal obligation: To comply with regulatory requirements

6. Data Sharing

6.1 Service providers:

  • Firebase (Google): Authentication, database, analytics, storage
  • OpenAI: AI query processing (anonymized data)
  • RevenueCat: Subscription and payment management
  • Email provider: Transactional email delivery

6.2 Other recipients:

  • Competent authorities upon legal request
  • Business partners (only with explicit consent)
  • In case of merger or acquisition

7. International Transfer

Some service providers operate outside the EU/US. We ensure such transfers occur with adequate safeguards:

  • EU Standard Contractual Clauses
  • European Commission adequacy decisions
  • Privacy Shield certifications (where applicable)
  • CCPA compliance for US users

8. Data Retention

  • Account data: Duration of account + 1 year
  • AI conversations: 2 years from last interaction
  • Analytics data: 14 months
  • Backups: 90 days
  • Security logs: 6 months

9. User Rights (GDPR & CCPA)

Under GDPR and CCPA, you have the right to:

  • Access: Request a copy of your personal data
  • Rectification: Correct inaccurate or incomplete data
  • Deletion: Request data deletion ("right to be forgotten")
  • Restriction: Limit processing in certain circumstances
  • Portability: Receive data in a structured, readable format
  • Object: Object to processing for direct marketing
  • Withdraw consent: At any time, without affecting prior processing legality
  • Non-discrimination (CCPA): Not be discriminated against for exercising rights

To exercise these rights, contact: privacy@greedandgross.com

10. Data Security

We implement technical and organizational security measures:

  • End-to-end encryption for sensitive data
  • HTTPS protocols for all communications
  • Two-factor authentication available
  • Regular security audits
  • Staff training on data protection
  • Encrypted and redundant backups

11. Cookies and Similar Technologies

We use cookies and similar technologies for:

  • Essential cookies: Necessary for app functionality
  • Analytics cookies: To understand app usage
  • Preference cookies: To remember your settings

12. Children

The App is intended exclusively for users 18 years and older. We do not knowingly collect data from minors. If we become aware of having collected data from minors, we will delete it immediately.

13. Privacy Policy Changes
