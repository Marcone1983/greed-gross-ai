How can I cancel my subscription?

You can cancel your subscription from your app store settings (Google Play or Apple App Store). For detailed assistance, contact us via email.

I can't access my account. What should I do?

1. Try resetting your password
2. Verify you're using the correct email
3. Check your internet connection
4. If the problem persists, contact us with your username

Is the app legal in my country?

The app provides educational content only. It's your responsibility to verify local cannabis laws. We cannot provide specific legal advice for every jurisdiction.

How can I request a refund?

Refunds follow app store policies. Generally, you have 48 hours to request a refund. Contact us for assistance with the process.

The AI provides incorrect information. How do I report it?

Report errors via email including:
- Screenshot of the conversation
- Date and time
- Error description
We'll investigate and correct promptly.
